# Procedures (Rust port)

Step-by-step procedures for ritualized actions during the port. Each file: one procedure, ordered steps, trigger condition.

## What belongs here

- Procedures with non-obvious steps or ordering
- Procedures where forgetting a step has cost (lost context, stale hand-offs, untracked scope drift)

## What does NOT belong here

- One-off action plans → charter or commit doc
- Format/template specifications → CLAUDE.md of the consumer (e.g., `../commits.kb/CLAUDE.md`)
- "What belongs here" / scoping guidance → CLAUDE.md of the relevant kb

# Decisions (Rust port)

Choices made for this port, with rationale. One file per decision: rationale, alternatives considered, trade-off accepted.

## What belongs here

- Normative choices for the port (language, crate architecture, dependencies, conventions)
- Trade-offs accepted and the alternatives rejected, briefly justified

## What does NOT belong here

- Facts / observable givens → `../facts.kb/`
- Plans for *future* decisions → charter or open hand-off
- Long-lived policy not specific to this port → elsewhere in `dev.kb/`

A decision should be *cite-able from a commit message*. That's the bar for granularity.
